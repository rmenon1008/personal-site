ny steam bus

Visit nysteamb.us for more.

The NY STEAM BUS aims supplement Science, Technology, Engineering, Art and Math (STEAM) education with exciting activities that grow a passion for these subjects. We retrofitted a school bus with floors, cabinets and computers to create the most versatile, mobile classroom. As the technology director, I focused on the initial design and retrofit of the bus and now work to integrate technology into our lessons to teach 21st century skills like programming and engineering.

Amid the COVID-19 pandemic, we started producing virtual STEAM activities that can be done at home for kids grade K-5. All these activities are public and can be found at our FlipGrid along with student video responses.
