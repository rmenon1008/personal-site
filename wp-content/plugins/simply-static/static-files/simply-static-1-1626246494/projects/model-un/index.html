Our Model United Nations club meets every Wednesday where we each represent a country, developing solutions to international problems. As the debate coordinator of the club, I write a background guide for each meeting that describes the issue that we will cover. Additionally, each year we bring a group of 13 to Philadelphia for ILMUNC, an international Model UN conference.
